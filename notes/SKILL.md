## memory.md — How to Add an Entry

Each entry follows this exact format:

```markdown
## Entry N
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Task:** One sentence describing what you were working on.
**Lesson:** One true insight — a real decision, mistake, or non-obvious discovery. Not a generic tip.
```

Rules:
- Date must be specific (not "recently" or "this week")
- Task must name the actual thing you did, not a category
- Lesson must be something that would have helped you avoid a mistake or make a better choice — if it reads like a textbook tip, rewrite it

## planning.md — How to Use It

This is the master planning document for the Silverleaf Academy media strategy. It has 9 stages:

1. Strategic Foundation
2. Content Pillar Framework
3. Never Miss Moments Library
4. Platform & Format Strategy
5. Monthly & Weekly Content Calendar
6. Shot Lists & Capture Checklists
7. Content Workflow & Operating System
8. Document Assembly & Formatting
9. Team Onboarding & Rollout

When working on a stage, read its **Actions**, complete them, then confirm the **Expected Outputs** exist before moving to the next stage.

---

## Gotchas

- Do not add generic filler entries to memory.md — the test is: "would this lesson have changed what I did?"
- planning.md is a living document for Silverleaf Academy — treat it as a real deliverable, not a template
